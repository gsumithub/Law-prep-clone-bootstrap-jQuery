About CLAT Exam

The Common Law Admission Test (CLAT) is a national-level entrance exam conducted annually for students seeking undergraduate law degrees in India. It is the primary gateway to securing a place in one of the 24 National Law Universities (NLUs), as well as various other leading law schools across the country. The CLAT tests students in areas such as legal reasoning, logical reasoning, English language, current affairs including general knowledge, and quantitative techniques.

Gateway to Top Law Colleges
